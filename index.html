About Us

Oodles Search Engine is a fun and easy way to explore websites you care about. It lets you create your own mini search engine—just pick a website and Oodles will scan it and build a searchable list of pages. You can then search through that list using simple keywords, like looking things up in a digital library you made yourself. No coding is needed and no setup is required. Whether you're researching a topic, organizing resources, or just curious, Oodles makes it easy to find what matters to you.

For more information, visit our Github page at https://github.com/stenoip/oodles.
