Welcome to the Oodles World Search by Stenoip Company!

This interactive interface allows you to search and explore the world in 3D. Here's how to get started:

  • Search: Type your query (e.g., "ice cream", "Walmart") at the top.
  • Move: Use the arrow buttons to move around the 3D world.
  • Look: Use the turn buttons on the right to rotate your view.
  • Visit: Search results appear as buildings—walk to one and click to visit the website.
