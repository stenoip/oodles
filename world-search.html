Welcome to the Oodles World Search by Stenoip Company!

This interactive interface allows you to search and explore the world in 3D. Here's how to get started:

  • Search: Type your query (e.g., "ice cream", "Walmart") in the search box at the top.
  • Move: Use the arrow buttons to move around the 3D world.
  • Look Around: Use the left and right turn buttons to look around in the world.
    •                
  • View Results: After entering your query, the search results will on "buildings". Go to them, click on them to visit website!

If you need more assistance, click the tutorial button again!
